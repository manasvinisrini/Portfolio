Short Summary

In the video below, one of the three Omni robots uses its Hokuyo URG-04lx-ug01 laser scanner to map out the NxR lab at Northwestern. The movement is controlled by a PS3 controller that is connected via Bluetooth to a 'master' computer. The 'master' computer then converts the PS3 command into a twist that is sent over a LAN to the robot. After Gmapping is complete, the robot can then use AMCL to accurately localize itself within the map as it moves.
